# The Changing Nature of Search

## Author
Rand Fishkin

## Source
https://www.linkedin.com/in/randfishkin/

## Post

Search behavior is evolving.

Users are getting answers without clicking.

This means:
- fewer organic clicks
- higher competition

Focus on brand + trust.

## Key Takeaways

- Zero-click searches are increasing
- Brand matters more than ever
- SEO is evolving beyond rankings
